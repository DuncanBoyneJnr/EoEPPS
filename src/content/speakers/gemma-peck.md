Gemma is Director at GTP Tech Recruitment and Co-Founder of Norfolk Power Platform User Group and East of England Power Platform Summit. With a background in mental health and psychotherapy, she brings empathy and insight to recruitment, supporting Microsoft Partners and professionals find roles where they can truly thrive. Passionate about work, wellbeing, strategic matchmaking, and building long-term relationships that go beyond placements. When she's not headhunting top talent, you'll find her adventuring in nature with her family and 2 kids.
